Leopards

Etymology

The English name "leopard" comes from Old French

Characteristics

The leopard's fur is generally soft and thick

Size

The leopard is a slender and muscular cat

Taxonomy

The leopard was designated as a type species of Panthera

Living subspecies

27 leopard subspecies were proposed by naturalists

Evolution

The last common ancestor of the Panthera lived long ago

Hybrids

A male leopard and a female lion were crossbred in Japan

Distribution and Habitat

The leopard has the largest distribution of all wild cats.

Behaviour and Ecology

The leopard is a solitary and territorial animal.
